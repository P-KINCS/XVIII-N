Revealed: The Bloody Countess

History named her a monster.
But behind the myth was a woman who stood too tall for her time.
Erzsébet Báthory was never given a fair trial — locked away without judgment,
silenced by fear, politics, and the threat she posed as a powerful woman.

Thank you for recognizing strength instead of fearing it —
for never trying to cage me,
for knowing that my power grows from your love,
and that everything I become, I become for us.

“Megtanultuk egymást.”
